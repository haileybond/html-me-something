My Mischievous Dachshunds

Making life interesting one mess at a time

Darcy

Darcy is my oldest (and fuzziest!) doxie pup. He's also the biggest trouble maker! He loves digging holes in the backyard, playing in the mud, swimming and fetching sticks in the water, playing fetch with his ball, going for hikes in the woods, and having his belly scratched. Most of all, he loves eating. He is fun, spunky, messy, and a very handsome fella.

Lizzie

Lizzie is our "middle child"" and she is a very unique little doxie. She is a tiny little girl weighing in at 8 pounds! She is very sweet and loves to snuggle with her humans and her little brother, Bingley. She loves kisses and does an enthusiatic dance every time mom and dad come home. She likes to wait for her brothers to finish their dinner before eating hers, so she can make them very jealous by eating slowly in front of them. She hates swimming but likes to put her paws in the water. She loves to chase ducks and her favorite thing to do is go for a hike with her family.
