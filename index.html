My Mischievous Dachshunds

Making life interesting one mess at a time

My Little Family

Hi, I'm Hailey! I'm a gal who nevers stops vacumming because I have three adorable, trouble-making, loving and playful miniature dachshunds who never stop shedding and making muddy messes (I have a husband too- and he also contributes to the need for constant vacuuming!)

Meet each of my pups below!

Darcy

Darcy is my oldest (and fuzziest!) doxie pup. He's also the biggest trouble maker! He loves digging holes in the backyard, playing in the mud, swimming and fetching sticks in the water, playing fetch with his ball, going for hikes in the woods, and having his belly scratched. Most of all, he loves eating. He is almost always stinky after playing and getting dirty in the rain, so he takes a lot of baths! He doesn't really mind bath time. He is a pretty freindly guy but he will bark at you if you stop petting him. (MORE PETS PLEASE!) He's been my partner in crime since 2011 and he made me fall in love with miniature dachshunds! Darcy is fun, spunky, messy, and a very handsome fella.

Lizzie

Lizzie is our "middle child"" and she is a very unique little doxie. She is a tiny little girl weighing in at 8 pounds! She is very sweet and loves to snuggle with her humans and her little brother, Bingley. She loves kisses and does an enthusiatic dance every time mom and dad come home. She likes to wait for her brothers to finish their dinner before eating hers, so she can make them very jealous by eating slowly in front of them. She hates swimming but likes to put her paws in the water. She loves to chase ducks and her favorite thing to do is go for a hike with her family. Her bark is shrill and piercing and she hates tiny humans (aka children) because she doesn't understand why they are so short. Strangers always think Lizzie is the cutest member of the gang, but then she scares them away.

Bingley

Bingley is our youngest and most energetic pup. He is the cutest little ball of blonde fuzz that you'll ever see. He's also the sweetest boy and his favorite thing to do is to snuggle with his daddy (or if dad isn't around, he'll settle for snuggles with mom). He loves chasing his little sister around, wrestling with his big brother Darcy and playing keep away with Darcy's favorite ball. He is learning to swim and he is terrible at walking on a leash. He throws a fit if mom and dad leave the house for more than 30 seconds and he needs attention and snuggles constantly. He's a little piggy and loves to eat. He's a clean freak like mom and spends a lot of his down time cleaning his paws (or his brother's ears, or his sister's teeth). He's an adorable little weirdo.
